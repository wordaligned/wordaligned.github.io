Code Craft

2007-01-03 • Content, C++, Python • Comments

Congratulations to Pete Goodliffe on his new book, Code Craft. Pete is one of the ACCU’s most consistent and consistently excellent writers. Over the years, his “Professionalism in Programming” column has provided lots of sound advice on, well, how to program, um, professionally.

The new book provides:

… language-agnostic advice that’s relevant to all developers …

but judging from the number of curly brackets on the (rather neat) front cover, and indeed in the sample chapter, I’d say it’s of most use to curly bracket programmers.
