What apple gets right

2007-09-07 • • Comments

I haven’t seen Windows Vista yet, let alone used it, and I’m in no particular hurry to, but I still enjoyed reading “What Vista Gets Right” over at Linux Magazine. Cunningly disguised as an honest account of which bits of Vista the Linux community would do well to steal, it’s actually an advert for Apple.

If there was a truth-in-advertising law, you’d see this in all Vista ads: “Try new Microsoft Vista! Now more Mac-like than ever!”

Tee hee!
